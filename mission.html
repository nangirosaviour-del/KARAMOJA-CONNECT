Our Strategy

Our mandate is driven by Ezekiel 36:24-36. Every mission involves:

  • Door-to-door youth evangelism.
  • Partnership with local Anglican Parish priests.
  • Physical needs assessment and support.
  • Establishing weekly fellowship roots in the community.
