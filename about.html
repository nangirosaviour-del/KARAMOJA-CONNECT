Who We Are

Karamoja Connect is a purposeful group that brings together born-again youths from Karamoja Anglican churches. We are united by a shared passion for ministry and outreach.


Our heart is to connect physically with all youths in Karamoja, connect them with the local church, and ultimately lead them to a lasting connection with Christ.

Our Core Pillars

Physical Connect

Meeting the youth where they are in their parishes.

Church Connect

Strengthening the bond between youth and the Anglican church.

Christ Connect

Leading souls to salvation and renewal.
